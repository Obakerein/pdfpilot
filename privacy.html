Privacy Policy

PDFPilot · Last updated: April 2026

PDFPilot processes all PDF files entirely on your device. Your files are never uploaded, never stored, and never leave your browser.

No Data Collection

PDFPilot does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or file content. Specifically:

  • Your PDF files are never uploaded to any server.
  • All processing happens locally in your browser using built-in JavaScript libraries.
  • We do not use analytics, telemetry, crash reporting, or any tracking tools.
  • We do not display ads or use advertising trackers.
  • We do not collect your name, email, IP address, or any other identifying information.

Permissions Used

  • offscreen — Used to run PDF processing (merging/splitting) in an isolated background context without any UI.
  • tabs — Used only to open the PDFPilot Editor and the Help/Welcome page when you request them.

Neither permission is used to read, monitor, or access any browser tabs or their content.

Local Storage

PDFPilot stores only your theme preference (light or dark mode) in your browser's local storage. This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

PDFPilot may load a font from Google Fonts. This request does not include any personal data or file content. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published here with a new date.
