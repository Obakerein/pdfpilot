Privacy Policy for PDFPilot

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

PDFPilot is a Chrome extension that allows users to merge, split, and reorder PDF files directly in their browser. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

No Data Collection

PDFPilot does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or user information.

Local Processing

All file processing is performed locally within your browser. Your PDF files are never uploaded to any server and never leave your device.

No Tracking

PDFPilot does not use analytics, tracking technologies, cookies, or any form of user monitoring.

No Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer any user data to third parties because no data is collected in the first place.

Permissions

PDFPilot operates entirely locally and does not require access to sensitive user data or external websites.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
